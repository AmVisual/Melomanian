Mystical horns

By playing a goat horn you can summon mystical creatures and phenomenons. Depending on a tune the goat horn emits you can get different results. Some can be useful, some adorable, and some can bring chaos to the Overworld. So remember to use them wisely. In order to summon creatures/phenomenons, you need an artefact (a necessary item). Some goat horns require to drop the items on the ground, some must be held in offhand. To summon, hold a goat horn in your mainhand and play it.
