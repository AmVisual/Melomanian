Powers

The power of music is Melomanian’s greatest strength. They can use it in several possible ways: playing a noteblock, blowing a goat horn or (their greatest magical ability) inserting a music disc into their enchanted jukebox stuck in their chests. If you want to learn how to use these abilities check the sections listed below.
